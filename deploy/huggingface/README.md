# Gaussian Random Field & Variogram Explorer

An interactive Shiny app for teaching and exploring variograms and Gaussian random fields.

## What you can do

- Choose from 12 covariance models (Matérn, Exponential, Gaussian, Spherical, and more)
- Adjust variance (σ²), scale (φ), nugget (τ²) and smoothness (κ)
- Simulate Gaussian random fields in 1D or 2D
- Compare empirical semivariograms against the theoretical curve
- Toggle Monte Carlo permutation envelopes
- Download the variogram plot
